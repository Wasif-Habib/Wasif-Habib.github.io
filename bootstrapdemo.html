Barrel cactus is a hardy desert plant known for its ribbed, rounded shape and ability to thrive with minimal water and care.

Rafflesia

Rafflesia is a rare parasitic plant known for producing the world’s largest flower and its distinct, foul odor that attracts pollinators. Also called a stinking corpse lily.

Lithops

Lithops, also known as "living stones," are small succulents that mimic rocks and require minimal water, making them ideal for low-maintenance indoor gardening.

Monkey Face Orchid

The Monkey Face Orchid is a rare, high-altitude flower known for its striking blooms that resemble a monkey’s face and its subtle, sweet fragrance.
